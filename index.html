Overview

From this research and statistics we see a growing demand for helping our aging population. Our solution satisfies that demand by helping the elderly live comfortably and longer through a monitoring service. The current market solutions are not feasible as they only contain single components like fall detection. Our solution gives way to a full system that detects fall detection, heart rate, location sensing, and environmental controls. It has the power to make elderly living more comfortable and secure.

Product Features

Heart Detection

Pulse will be continuously measured from the pulse sensor. The sensor will be outside our system/chest piece and always in contact with the skin to measure the pulse. The data will be collected and stored to our main system in the home. This data will help determine emergency situations and when to place phone calls. The primary reason for using pulse is to measure heart attacks and elevated heart scenarios.

Fall Detection

It will operate all the time when consumer wears the device. It works by sensing the forces acting on the chest unit and if any big deviations were to occur, a huge spike would result. If the person is unable to respond to the automatic phone call, immediate assistance will be sent. Also, if the system does not detect any movement for an extended period of time, a phone call to the user will be made. This is primarily for detecting unconscious behavior and falls to the ground (account for no movement).

Home Control

The system will provide a small degree of environmental control. This will be provided by voice commands received by a small microphone on the chest unit, and the commands will be processed and appropriate actions will be taken. Example controls would include turning on and off lights, or the television.
