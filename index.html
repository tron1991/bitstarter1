From this research and statistics we see a growing demand for helping our aging population. Our solution satisfies that demand by helping the elderly live comfortably and longer through a monitoring service. The current market solutions are not feasible as they only contain single components like fall detection. Our solution gives way to a full system that detects fall detection, heart rate, location sensing, and environmental controls. It has the power to make elderly living more comfortable and secure.

Product Features

Heart Detection

Your heart is monitored with NANA. We measure heart signals for any inaccuracies and if large deviations occur an emergency distress call is sent.

In-Home Location

With NANA your loved one is safe. We offer safety in the form of location sensing within the home. RFID tags will be dispersed throughout the home, and when NANA wereable is near location it will signal the base station. Properties like time, location, and mapping will be displayed.

Fall Detection

When you fall, you appreciate a helping hand. NANA is that hand to help loved ones during these unpredictable falls.

Emergency Prevention

When you are in an emergency situation, NANA is equipped to send distress calls to our monitoring station. NANA watches for elevation in heart levels, fall alerts, or simply staying in one room to long.

Environmental Control

NANA is the first wearable device to control appliances within your home through voice or base station activation. Quite useful for our elderly citizens.

Voice Integration

"Would you like me to turn on your lights", said NANA. You can speak commands for basic usage or through emergency situations.

Research Testing with NANA

NANA validated by Elderly

NANA had the privilege to work with research participants in our latest prototype. In our research we tested the following: heart measurements for accuracy, increased movement with heart accuracies, and an emergency situation. All situations were successful with NANA. We test normal breathing for our heart sensor accuracy. We perform the "Sit up Sit down" test as it will portray elderly movements accurately. Lasty, a simple squat exercise proves that NANA can detect a fall. This wearable technology is truly amazing.

NANA turns appliances ON

NANA is not only a passive device, but active for the elderly person. NANA can turn appliances on/off with voice command as shown in the YouTube video. Elderly loose their motor movements which could impair their daily lives. With NANA, simple tasks around the house can be completed with little or no effort.
